TEXTILE

My first research is to convert my digital compositions
to textile images. These are made by means of spray paint
to spray on templates, and to put them into compositions.

This remains very flat, that is why I started screen-printing with different types of digital structures, because of the combination there is more depth in the work. In addition to silkscreen printing, I have also converted the digital images into jacquard fabrics.
